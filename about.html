I'm Rachel, a trilingual user and consumer experience designer, and design event organizer, based out of Montreal. After 15 years of pushing pixels and pulling anchor-points within the retail, CPG and branding space at Peter Schmidt Group and Pahnke, I'm focusing all my energies to my favorite part of design - the “why.”

I grew up in New York, studied in Michigan, and worked and lived in Atlanta, Ga, Hamburg, Germany and now Montreal, Quebec. Immersing myself in various cultures has taught me to listen intently, observe intensely and explore constantly. This curiosity and humility are what drew me to the strategic nature of User Experience Design.

I started working in design agencies in 2009 and have collaborated and worked with clients such as Coca-Cola, Jim Beam, The YMCA and Werther's Originals.

Many of these works are undisclosed due to NDA agreements - reach out for more information.
